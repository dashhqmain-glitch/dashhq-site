Questions

Before you apply.

How do I become a Dash Citizen?+
To become a Dash Citizen, submit your application through our official website. All applications are reviewed by the founder, who personally selects members to maintain the quality and alignment of the community.
Is there a fee to join?+
No. Membership as a Dash Citizen is completely free — there are no joining fees or subscription costs.
What exactly do I get access to?+
As a Dash Citizen, you receive high-quality alpha and early insights, access to our exclusive tools and resources, and entry into a private, high-signal community of talented traders, builders and Web3 enthusiasts.
Do I need to be an experienced trader?+
You don't need to be a professional trader, but we recommend at least an intermediate-level understanding of Web3 and NFTs. This ensures you can actively participate and get the most value from the group.
How active is the community?+
Very active. Dash Citizens are known for consistent daily engagement, alpha sharing and collaborative opportunities — making it one of the most active communities in the space.
